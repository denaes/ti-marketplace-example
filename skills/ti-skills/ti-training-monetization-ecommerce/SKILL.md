# Thought Industries — training monetization & eCommerce

## Purpose

Summarize Thought Industries’ **public** monetization narrative: native eCommerce to make training a **revenue driver**, with flexible purchase models and global reach. Use for **packaging, pricing motions, and stakeholder communication**—not as a substitute for contract or SKU truth.

## When to Use

- Designing SKUs: subscriptions vs bundles vs events
- B2B vs B2C or mixed models
- International pricing, tax, and currency questions at a high level
- Explaining Panorama in the context of licensing to enterprises or resellers

## Public capabilities (themes)

| Theme | Details called out on TI monetization pages |
|-------|---------------------------------------------|
| **Payment flexibility** | Subscriptions, bulk or à la carte purchases, training credits, discount codes |
| **Packaging** | Subscriptions, collections, bundles of **courses and events**; individuals, groups, organizations; license or purchase |
| **B2B & B2C** | eCommerce engine supports both |
| **Global** | Many currencies, multiple tax providers, multi-currency purchases |
| **Licensing & audiences** | License content for enterprises, distributors, resellers; ties to Panorama / multi-tenancy story |
| **Gateways** | Shopping cart integrates with gateways such as Stripe, Worldpay, Cybersource, Chase Paymentech; can connect to **own** eCommerce infrastructure |

## Instructions

1. **Clarify the commercial motion.** Subscription vs one-time vs credits vs enterprise deal—TI’s story supports multiple; the user must pick what applies.
2. **Separate list price from entitlement.** Packaging (collections/bundles) is a merchandising concern; entitlements and audience segmentation belong with distribution/personalization skills.
3. **Global rollout.** If the user mentions multiple countries, surface currency, tax, and payment method needs at a **requirements** level; implementation details require their TI configuration and integrations.
4. **Avoid legal/financial certainty.** Tax and payment compliance vary by jurisdiction; recommend professional review for production decisions.
5. **Cross-reference analytics.** Tie monetization KPIs (attach rate, ARPU, refund/chargeback) to `ti-learning-data-analytics-roi` when defining success.

## Quality checklist

- [ ] Purchase model(s) named explicitly
- [ ] Group/org vs individual buying addressed when B2B is in scope
- [ ] No unsupported claims about fees, supported countries, or gateway contracts
